Bios

I am currently a final-year Ph.D. student from Department of ECE at University of Alberta, Canada (UoA) advised by Professor Li Cheng. During my Ph.D., I was fortunate to intern at Meta Reality Labs for two times, advised Dr. Yuanlu Xu in 2023 and Dr. Minh Vo in 2022 and also Huawei 2012 Labs for two times. Before Ph.D., I graduated from University College London (UCL) with a MRes degree in Department of Computer Science advised by Professor Jun Wang and Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) with a BSc degree advised by Professor Huiqi Li.

My research interests lie in computer vision, machine learning, and reinforcement learning.

  • Digital Humans: motion capture system, human pose estimation, shape reconstruction, and motion prediction.
  • AI Generated Content (AIGC) in human modeling and beyond: text2motion generation, deffusion model, clothed human modeling.
  • Learning in Computer Vision and Novel Sensors: event camera, polarization camera, spiking neural networks, transformer, self-supervised and unsupervised learning.

As I am near the completion of my PhD, I will join Multimedia Lab Multimedia Lab at SAIT, as a branch of Multimedia Lab at Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), was founded by Professor Xiaoou Tang and Yu Qiao, and is currently led by Professor Chao Dong. at Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (SAIT) as a Research Assisstant Professor soon. If you are interested in collaborating on research or have any other inquiries, please don't hesitate to contact me. Thank you for visiting!
